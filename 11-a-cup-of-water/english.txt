Hi, I’m Emma. Let me share one small habit that helps me every day.

Each morning, I drink a glass of water after I get out of bed.

The water is cool and fresh, and it helps me wake up slowly.

I keep a blue cup beside the sink, so it is easy to remember.

I fill the cup, take a small sip, and enjoy the quiet morning.

Drinking water makes my mouth feel fresh and my body feel ready.

I also carry a small bottle when I go to school or work.

When I feel tired, I stop for a moment and take a drink.

This short break helps me slow down and begin again with more energy.

I do not need a special drink or a difficult plan.

Clean water and a few quiet seconds are enough for this simple habit.

At night, I wash my cup and put it back by the sink.

Then it is ready for me when a new morning begins.

One small glass of water can be a kind start to your day.
