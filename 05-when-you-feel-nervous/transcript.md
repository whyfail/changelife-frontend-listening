# When You Feel Nervous

- Level: CEFR A2 / US elementary grades 4-6
- English words: 220
- Voice: Emma, American English
- Duration: about 2 minutes

## Bilingual Transcript

Sometimes we feel nervous before a test, a game, or even a short talk with someone.

有时候，我们在考试前、比赛前，甚至和别人简短交谈时都会感到紧张。

It is a perfectly normal feeling, and everyone feels this way from time to time.

这是一种完全正常的感受，每个人都会时不时地经历它。

When that happens, I want to share a simple but very helpful tip with you today.

当那种情况发生时，我想今天和你分享一个简单但非常有帮助的小技巧。

First, try slow breathing. Close your eyes gently and breathe in slowly through your nose.

首先，尝试慢呼吸。轻轻地闭上眼睛，然后慢慢地通过鼻子吸气。

Then breathe out slowly through your mouth, as if you are blowing out a small candle.

然后慢慢用嘴呼气，就像在吹灭一支小蜡烛。

This helps your body relax. Your heart beats more slowly, and your mind feels much clearer.

这会帮助身体放松，让心跳慢下来，头脑也变得更加清晰。

Next, do one small action that feels very easy and comfortable for you right now.

接下来，做一个你觉得现在非常容易且舒适的小动作。

It could be standing up, stretching your arms, or simply smiling at yourself in the mirror.

可以是站起来、伸展手臂，或者只是对着镜子里的自己微笑。

That little movement tells your brain that you are safe, strong, and ready to move forward.

这个小动作会告诉大脑：你很安全、很坚强，也准备好继续向前。

You can try this before a test, a game, or any moment when you feel really worried.

你可以在考试前、比赛前，或在任何你感到真正担心的时刻尝试这个方法。

With practice, this will soon start to feel more natural and easier.

多练习几次，这个方法很快就会变得更自然、更容易。

Remember, feeling nervous does not mean something is wrong with you or that you are weak at all.

记住，感到紧张并不意味着你有什么问题，也绝不代表你软弱。

Slow breathing and one small action together can really make a big difference in how you feel.

慢呼吸和一个小动作结合在一起，真的可以对你的感觉产生很大的不同。

You are brave and capable, and you can handle it one breath at a time.

你很勇敢，也很有能力，可以一次呼吸、一步一步地面对它。
