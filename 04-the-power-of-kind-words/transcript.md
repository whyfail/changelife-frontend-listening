# The Power of Kind Words

- Level: CEFR A2 / US elementary grades 4-6
- English words: 229
- Voice: Emma, American English
- Duration: about 2 minutes

## Bilingual Transcript

I love noticing how a few simple words can turn a gray day into something brighter.

我喜欢注意到几句简单的话如何能把灰暗的一天变得明亮起来。

When I told my friend her hair looked nice, she smiled and the whole room felt warmer.

当我告诉朋友她的头发很好看时，她笑了，整个房间都感到更温暖。

Saying thank you to the cashier after a busy morning always makes me feel more connected.

在忙碌的早晨后对收银员说声谢谢，总让我感到更加亲近。

Kind words are like little rays of sunlight. They can lift a heavy heart without much effort.

善意的话语就像一缕缕阳光，不费多少力气就能让沉重的心轻松起来。

Many people walk around feeling unseen, but a simple hello can remind them they matter.

许多人在周围走动时感到被忽视，但一句简单的问候可以提醒他们，他们很重要。

I try to start each day with one warm phrase, such as, "Have a nice day."

我会试着每天先说一句温暖的话，比如“祝你今天愉快”。

It does not have to sound perfect. Being honest and kind is what makes the words feel good.

这句话不必听起来很完美，真诚和善意才会让人感到温暖。

When we share kindness, relationships grow warmer, like a fire that spreads gently across a room.

当我们分享善意时，关系会变得更温暖，就像火焰在房间里轻轻蔓延。

I remember a stranger once told me, "You are doing great," and those words lifted my whole afternoon.

我记得有个陌生人曾对我说“你做得很好”，那句话点亮了我整个下午。

That tiny comment reminded me that a few seconds of encouragement can echo for hours.

那句小小的鼓励让我意识到，几秒钟的激励可以回荡数小时。

When we speak kindly, the listener’s mood often brightens, and sometimes they pass that light on.

当我们友善地说话时，听者的情绪往往会变得明朗，有时他们还会把那份光亮传递下去。

I challenge myself to notice one opportunity each day to say something gentle and true.

我挑战自己每天留意一次机会，说一句温和而真诚的话。

It can be for a family member, a friend, or even someone I pass on the street.

这句话可以送给家人、朋友，甚至是我在街上遇到的人。

Remember, your kind words are like small seeds. They can grow into warm and lasting connections.

记住，你善意的话语就像小种子，可以长成温暖而长久的关系。
