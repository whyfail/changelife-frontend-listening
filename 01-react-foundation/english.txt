Mia: Yes, all three are owned by the independent foundation, not by Meta any more, right now.

Mia: Think of it like a school that has its own building instead of sharing a big office.

Mia: They’re like eight friends who each help keep the project running smoothly.

Ben: And the technical choices still come from the React contributors and maintainers, right?

Mia: Yes, the foundation gives them resources and guidance, but the code decisions stay with the community.

Ben: That keeps the spirit of open source alive while offering more stability.

Mia: Think of it like a sports team that gets its own stadium and budget, but the coaches still pick the plays.

Ben: I’m curious how this will affect React Native and JSX in future updates, and what new features we might see.

Mia: The foundation says the roadmap stays the same, just with more support from the members, and better funding for the teams.
