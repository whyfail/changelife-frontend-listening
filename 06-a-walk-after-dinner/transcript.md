# A Walk After Dinner

- Level: CEFR A2 / US elementary grades 4-6
- English words: 244
- Voice: Emma, American English
- Duration: about 2 minutes

## Bilingual Transcript

After dinner, I like to step outside and take a short walk around the neighborhood while the sky turns golden and the stars appear.

晚饭后，我喜欢走出去，在附近散一会儿步，此时天空变成金色，星星出现。

The air is cooler, the street lights are on, and everything feels calm as the day ends.

空气变得凉爽，路灯亮起，一切在白昼结束时显得宁静。

I start slowly, letting my feet find an easy rhythm as I feel the gentle breeze.

我慢慢起步，让双脚找到轻松的节奏，同时感受柔和的微风。

Sometimes I listen to soft music, sometimes I just hear the night sounds like crickets chirping.

有时我听轻柔的音乐，有时我只听夜晚的声音，像蟋蟀在鸣叫。

A gentle walk helps my body digest the food I just ate more comfortably.

轻松的散步帮助我的身体更舒适地消化刚吃的食物。

It also gives my mind a chance to settle after a busy day and let go of my worries.

它也让我的思绪在忙碌的一天后平静下来，放下心里的烦恼。

I notice how my breath becomes slower and my thoughts grow clearer as I walk.

我注意到，随着散步，我的呼吸变慢，思绪也变得更清晰。

If I feel tense, I focus on the rhythm of my steps and let that feeling go.

如果我感到紧张，就把注意力放在脚步的节奏上，让那种感觉慢慢离开。

When I return home, I feel lighter, both in body and in spirit, ready for a restful night.

回到家时，我感到身心都轻盈，准备好迎接宁静的夜晚。

This small habit does not take much time. Ten or fifteen minutes fit easily into my evening.

这个小习惯不需要太多时间，十到十五分钟就能轻松融入我的夜晚。

I have found that a short walk can turn a tired evening into a peaceful one by calming my thoughts.

我发现短暂的散步可以通过平复思绪，把疲惫的夜晚变成宁静的夜晚。

It reminds me that caring for myself can be as easy as stepping outside and enjoying simple moments.

它提醒我，关爱自己可以像走出去享受简单的时刻一样容易。

I hope you will try a quiet walk after dinner soon and feel the calm it brings.

我希望你很快尝试在晚饭后安静地散步，感受它带来的平静。

Remember, a few minutes of gentle movement can brighten your whole evening and lift your mood.

记住，几分钟的轻柔活动可以点亮你整个夜晚，提升你的情绪。
