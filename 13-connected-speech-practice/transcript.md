# Connected Speech Practice

- Level: CEFR A1-A2 pronunciation practice
- Exercises: 40
- Voice: Emma, American English
- Duration: 5.6 minutes
- Pattern: clear full form once, natural spoken form twice

## Bilingual Transcript

Listen to the clear sentence first. Then hear natural American speech two times.

先听清晰完整的句子，再听两遍自然美式口语。

Part one. Four easy examples.

第一部分：四个入门例子。

Clear: Because I love you.  Natural: Because I love you.

因为我爱你。because 的非重读音节会弱化。

Clear: You can do it.  Natural: You can do it.

你能做到。肯定句中的 can 常弱读为 /kən/。

Clear: What did he say?  Natural: What did he say?

他说了什么？did he 连读时，he 的 h 可能弱化。

Clear: What are you doing?  Natural: Whaddaya doing?

你在做什么？what are you 会连续弱读和连读。

Part two. Weak sounds.

第二部分：高频弱读。

Clear: I want to go.  Natural: I wanna go.

我想走。to 在非重读位置常弱读，want to 常连成 wanna。

Clear: This is for you.  Natural: This is fer ya.

这是给你的。for 和 you 都会弱读。

Clear: You and me.  Natural: You 'n me.

你和我。and 常弱读到只剩 /n/。

Clear: A cup of tea.  Natural: A cup uh tea.

一杯茶。of 常弱读为 /əv/，有时更接近 /ə/。

Clear: I can do it.  Natural: I c'n do it.

我能做到。肯定句中的 can 通常不重读。

Clear: Do you know?  Natural: D'ya know?

你知道吗？you 常弱读为 /jə/。

Clear: Tell him to come.  Natural: Tell 'im ta come.

叫他来。him 的 h 可能省略，to 同时弱读。

Clear: Give her a call.  Natural: Give 'er a call.

给她打个电话。her 的 h 可能省略。

Part three. Linking and sound changes.

第三部分：连读、同化与省音。

Clear: Did you see it?  Natural: Didja see it?

你看见了吗？did you 常听起来像 didja。

Clear: Would you help me?  Natural: Wouldja help me?

你愿意帮我吗？would you 常发生同化。

Clear: Could you open it?  Natural: Couldja open it?

你能打开它吗？could you 常发生同化。

Clear: Don't you know?  Natural: Dontcha know?

你不知道吗？don't you 常听起来像 dontcha。

Clear: Can't you see?  Natural: Cantcha see?

你看不见吗？can't you 常听起来像 cantcha。

Clear: What do you want?  Natural: Whaddaya want?

你想要什么？what do you 会连续弱读和连读。

Clear: What are you doing?  Natural: Whaddaya doing?

你在做什么？are 和 you 都会弱读并连在一起。

Clear: Does he know?  Natural: Does 'e know?

他知道吗？does he 连读时 h 可能弱化。

Clear: Is he coming?  Natural: Is 'e coming?

他来吗？is he 连读时 h 可能弱化。

Clear: Tell him the truth.  Natural: Tell 'im the truth.

告诉他真相。tell him 连读时 him 的 h 可能省略。

Part four. Common short forms.

第四部分：常见固定缩读。

Clear: I'm going to leave.  Natural: I'm gonna leave.

我要走了。going to 常缩读为 gonna。

Clear: Do you want to go?  Natural: D'ya wanna go?

你想去吗？want to 常缩读为 wanna。

Clear: I've got to go.  Natural: I gotta go.

我得走了。got to 常缩读为 gotta。

Clear: I have to work.  Natural: I hafta work.

我必须工作。have to 常听起来像 hafta。

Clear: She has to leave.  Natural: She has ta leave.

她必须离开。has to 在自然口语中会弱化。

Clear: Let me see.  Natural: Lemme see.

让我看看。let me 常缩读为 lemme。

Clear: Give me a minute.  Natural: Gimme a minute.

给我一分钟。give me 常缩读为 gimme。

Clear: It's kind of strange.  Natural: It's kinda strange.

这有点奇怪。kind of 常缩读为 kinda。

Clear: We're out of time.  Natural: We're outta time.

我们没时间了。out of 常缩读为 outta。

Clear: I have a lot of work.  Natural: I have a lotta work.

我有很多工作。a lot of 常连成 a lotta。

Clear: You should have called.  Natural: You shoulda called.

你本应该打电话。should have 常缩读为 shoulda。

Clear: I could have helped.  Natural: I coulda helped.

我本可以帮忙。could have 常缩读为 coulda。

Clear: I would have stayed.  Natural: I woulda stayed.

我本来会留下。would have 常缩读为 woulda。

Part five. Full sentence practice.

第五部分：整句听感训练。

Clear: What do you want to do?  Natural: Whaddaya wanna do?

你想做什么？

Clear: Did you tell him about it?  Natural: Didja tell 'im about it?

你把这件事告诉他了吗？

Clear: I should have told you.  Natural: I shoulda told ya.

我本应该告诉你。

Clear: Do you want to go with me?  Natural: D'ya wanna go with me?

你想和我一起去吗？

Clear: What are you doing?  Natural: Whaddaya doing?

你在做什么？
