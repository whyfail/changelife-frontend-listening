# Believe in Yourself

- Level: CEFR A2 / US elementary grades 4-6
- English words: 230
- Voice: Emma, American English
- Duration: about 2 minutes

## Bilingual Transcript

Hi, I’m Emma. Today I want to talk about something that helps us every day.

大家好，我是艾玛。今天我想和大家聊聊一件每天都会帮助我们的事。

It’s called confidence, the feeling that we can try new things even when they seem hard.

这叫自信，一种即使事情看起来困难，我们仍敢于尝试的感觉。

Confidence doesn’t come from being perfect; it grows when we keep trying and never give up.

自信不是来自完美，而是来自我们不断尝试、永不放弃的过程中慢慢增长的。

When we make a mistake, we can ask ourselves what we learned and how to improve.

当我们犯错时，可以问自己学到了什么，怎样可以做得更好。

That small question turns a failure into a step forward, and our confidence gets a little stronger.

这个小问题把失败变成前进的一步，让我们的自信变得更强大一点。

It also helps to notice the progress we make, even if it seems tiny at first.

注意到自己取得的进步也很有帮助，哪怕一开始看起来微不足道。

Maybe we wrote a sentence without a spelling error, or we finished a short exercise on time.

也许我们写了一个没有拼写错误的句子，或者按时完成了简短的练习。

Those little wins remind us that we are getting better, and that feeling is truly encouraging.

这些小小的胜利提醒我们正在变得更好，那种感觉真的很鼓舞人心。

When we feel a bit proud, we can share it with a friend or write it in a small journal.

当我们感到一点自豪时，可以和朋友分享，或者把这些进步写在小本子里。

Writing down progress makes it easier to look back and see how far we have come.

把进步写下来，让我们回头看时能清楚地看到自己走过的路。

Remember, every expert was once a beginner who practiced a little each day with patience.

记住，每位专家曾经都是每天坚持练习一点点、耐心学习的初学者。

So if a task feels tough, take a deep breath, try one small step, and give it a chance.

所以如果任务觉得很难，深呼吸，尝试迈出小一步，给它一个机会。

Soon you will notice more confidence bubbling up inside you, like a warm sunrise.

很快你会感受到更多的自信在内心涌动，像温暖的朝阳一样。

You are enough, and every effort you make is proof of your growing strength.

你已经足够好了，你付出的每一次努力都证明你正在变得更强大。
