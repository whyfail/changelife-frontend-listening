# Making a New Friend

- Level: CEFR A2 / US elementary grades 4-6
- English words: 220
- Voice: Emma, American English
- Duration: about 2 minutes

## Bilingual Transcript

I used to feel shy when I met new people, but I learned that a smile can change everything right away.

我以前见到陌生人时会害羞，但我现在明白，一个微笑就能立刻改变一切。

A smile costs nothing, yet it tells someone, "You are welcome here," and opens a door.

微笑不花一分钱，却能告诉别人“这里欢迎你”，也能打开交流的大门。

Then I say a simple hello, just one word, and it feels like a warm, bright invitation.

然后我说一句简单的“你好”，只有一个词，却像一份温暖而明亮的邀请。

After that, I ask a sincere question, like, "What do you like to do for fun?"

之后，我会真诚地问一个问题，比如“你平时喜欢做什么？”

That question shows I truly care and want to know more about that person.

这个问题表明我真的在乎，并且想更多地了解那个人。

I try this at school, in the library, or even at the playground after class.

我会在学校、图书馆，甚至下课后的操场上这样和别人打招呼。

One day I saw a boy sitting alone, quietly reading a comic, and I smiled at him.

有一天，我看到一个男孩独自坐着，安静地看漫画，于是我对他笑了笑。

He looked up. I said hello and asked, "What is your favorite comic right now?"

他抬起头，我说了声“你好”，然后问他现在最喜欢哪本漫画。

His face lit up, he smiled back, and we started talking about heroes and adventures.

他的脸上露出光彩，笑着回应，我们于是聊起了英雄和冒险的故事。

Soon we were sharing snacks, laughing at jokes, and making plans to play together.

很快我们就一起分享零食，笑谈笑话，还计划一起玩耍。

Having a new friend helps me feel less lonely, more cheerful, and more confident each day.

拥有一位新朋友，让我每天都不再那么孤单，心情更开朗，也更有信心。

Sometimes it is hard, and people may not answer, but I keep trying.

有时候会遇到困难，别人可能不回应，但我会继续尝试。

I start with one smile, one hello, one kind question, and I see the world grow friendlier.

我从一个微笑、一句你好、一个友善的问题开始，看到世界变得更有爱。

Remember, every smile you give can become the first step toward a wonderful friendship.

记住，你送出的每一个微笑，都可能成为一段美好友谊的第一步。
