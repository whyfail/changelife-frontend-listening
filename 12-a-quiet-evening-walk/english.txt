After dinner, I like to take a short and quiet walk near my home.

I put on my comfortable shoes, open the front door, and step outside.

The evening air feels cool, and the street looks calm under the soft lights.

I walk slowly because I do not need to hurry or go very far.

First, I pass a small shop with warm yellow lights in the window.

Then I see tall trees, parked cars, and people walking with their dogs.

Sometimes a neighbor smiles and says hello when we meet on the street.

I smile back, and that small friendly moment makes me feel happy.

I can hear birds in the trees and dry leaves under my feet.

I look at the sky and watch the last light of the day.

For a few minutes, I forget my phone, my work, and my busy plans.

I simply look around, breathe slowly, and enjoy the place where I live.

Soon I turn around and walk back home with a warm and quiet mind.

A simple walk can make an ordinary evening feel peaceful and good.
